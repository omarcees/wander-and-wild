Who We Are

Born from a love of
uncharted roads

Wander & Wild was founded by three friends who quit their jobs, sold their cars, and spent a year travelling across six continents. What they discovered changed everything — travel, done slowly and intentionally, has the power to transform.

Today we help thousands of travellers design experiences that go beyond sightseeing. Fewer crowds, deeper culture, and memories that actually last.
